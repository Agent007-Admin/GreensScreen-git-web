05 · Usage guidelines — do's & don'ts
DO
◆Use the primary logo on dark or black backgrounds
◆Maintain clear space equal to the height of the "G"
◆Use monochrome versions on light or print surfaces
◆Use the square icon for Discord, GitHub, and app icons
◆Keep the ENT tag and signal dot together at all times
◆Use Bebas Neue exclusively for the wordmark
DON'T
✕Stretch, skew, or distort the logo proportions
✕Place the logo on busy backgrounds without a dark overlay
✕Swap Bebas Neue for any other typeface
✕Use off-brand colors on the wordmark
✕Remove the ENT designation or signal dot
✕Recreate the logo by hand — always use the SVG files
USE WHEN
→Full stacked → merch, thumbnails, stream alerts, posters
→Horizontal → email headers, stream overlays, banners
→Square icon → Discord, GitHub, app icons, favicon
→Primary green → all digital, screens, dark backgrounds
→Monochrome → print, embroidery, watermarks, merch tags
→Accent lime → highlights, CTAs, secondary callouts
