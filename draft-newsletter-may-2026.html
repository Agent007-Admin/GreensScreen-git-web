01
Hardware Announced: May 15, 2026
ASUS ROG Strix SCAR 18 (2026)

The world's first 18-inch 4K 240Hz Mini-LED laptop panel redefines portable flagship performance.

RESEARCH_DIGEST

The 2026 ROG Strix SCAR 18 redefines flagship gaming performance by featuring the world's first 18-inch 4K 240Hz Mini-LED panel with ROG Nebula ELMB technology. Under the hood, it integrates the latest Intel Core Ultra 9 290HX Plus processor and the NVIDIA RTX 5090 Laptop GPU, delivering an incredible 320W of sustained total system power. Visual supremacy is achieved through 2304 local dimming zones and a peak brightness of 1600 nits, while thermal management is handled by a full-surround vapor chamber. This machine is engineered for elite performance in 4K gaming and complex AI-focused workloads.

02
GPU Update News: April 20, 2026
NVIDIA RTX 5060 9GB Transition

Mid-range GPU production shifts to 9GB GDDR7 modules following Blackwell's successful architecture rollout.

RESEARCH_DIGEST

NVIDIA has officially transitioned its mid-range GPU production to the RTX 5060 9GB GDDR7 variant following the successful launch of the Blackwell architecture. This new SKU utilizes three 3GB memory modules on a 96-bit bus, offering a more efficient memory footprint despite the narrower bandwidth compared to previous-generation cards. The transition is designed to stabilize global supply chains while introducing AI-accelerated features like DLSS 4.5 to the mass market. Targeted at high-refresh 1440p gaming, the card provides significant architectural improvements in power efficiency and tensor core throughput for next-gen rendering.
